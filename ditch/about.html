About / Contact

D-itch is a collective effort to put forth discussions on contemporary topics relating to technology, surveillance, cultural theory and other adjacent topics. Currently composed of Antoine Habib and François Osborne. Both pursuing higher education in applied mathematics at the Polytechnique of Montreal and plasma physics at the University of Glasgow, respectively.

Contributions to the project are always welcomed, be they writing, visual media, auditive pieces or any other form relevant to the topics discussed.

For any inquiries please contact: digital.itch@outlook.com.
